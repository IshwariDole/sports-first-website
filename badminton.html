Badminton

Badminton is a popular sport played worldwide.

How to Play Badminton ?


  • Two players or two pairs (doubles) on each side
  • Objective: score points by hitting the shuttlecock over the net
  • Players use rackets to hit the shuttlecock back and forth
  • Points are scored when the opponent fails to return the shuttlecock
  • Game is played in sets (usually best of 3)
  • Each game is played to 21 points (must win by 2)
