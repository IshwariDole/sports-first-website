Football

Football is a popular sport played worldwide.

How to Play Football ?


  • Two teams of 11 players each
  • Objective: score goals
  • Use feet, head, or body (no hands except goalkeeper)
  • Match has two 45-minute halves
  • Game starts with a kick-off
  • Ball must cross the goal line to score
  • Fouls lead to free kicks or penalties
  • Team with more goals wins
