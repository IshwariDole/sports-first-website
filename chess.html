Chess

Chess is a strategic board game played worldwide.

How to Play Chess ?


  • Two players with 16 pieces each
  • Objective: checkmate the opponent's king
  • Players alternate turns moving one piece at a time
  • Pieces have specific movement rules (e.g., rooks move in straight lines)
  • Special moves include castling and en passant
  • Game ends when a king is checkmated or players agree to a draw
