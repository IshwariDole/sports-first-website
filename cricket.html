Cricket

Cricket is a popular sport played worldwide.

How to Play Cricket ?


  • Two teams of 11 players each
  • Objective: score runs
  • One team bats while the other bowls and fields
  • Batsmen score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets
  • Bowler delivers the ball to the batsman
  • Fielding team tries to get batsmen out through various methods
  • Match formats: Test (5 days), ODI (50 overs), T20 (20 overs)
  • Team with most runs wins
